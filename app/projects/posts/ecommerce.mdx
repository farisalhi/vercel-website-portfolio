As part of a team of three, I developed a full-stack e-commerce platform that included both web and mobile applications. We built the backend using Django with a MySQL database to manage the product catalog, users, and transactions, while also designing a clean, intuitive frontend aligned with Material Design principles to ensure a modern user experience. The platform featured wishlists, shopping carts, and a personalized recommendation algorithm, along with an integrated AI chatbot to enhance interactivity and customer support. To extend functionality across devices, we also developed a companion Android mobile app, creating a seamless cross-platform shopping experience.
