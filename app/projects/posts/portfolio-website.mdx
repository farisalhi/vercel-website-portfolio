This is a project which you are using right now! I developed this website to showcase my projects, hobbies, and me as a whole. I wanted to create a space where I could share my thoughts, experiences, and projects with the world, while also learning and growing as a developer and as a person. I went through several iterations of the website, starting with a simple HTML and CSS website, then moving on to a React website hosted with AWS Amplify, and finally to this website, which is a Next.js website hosted with Vercel. I learned a lot about web development, design, and myself throughout this process, and I'm excited to see where this website will take me in the future. I hope you enjoy your time here, and if you have any feedback or suggestions, feel free to reach out to me! I'm always looking to improve and grow, and I would love to hear from you.
